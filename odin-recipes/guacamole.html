Guacamole Recipe

Description

Ingredients

  • 3 avocados
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 3 tbsp cilantro
  • 2 plum tomatoes, diced
  • 1 tsp minced garlic
  • 1 pinch cayenne pepper

Directions

  1. Mash together the avocados, lime juice, and salt into medium bowl
  2. Mix in onion, cilantro, tomatoes, and garlic
  3. Refrigerate 1 hour for best flavour
